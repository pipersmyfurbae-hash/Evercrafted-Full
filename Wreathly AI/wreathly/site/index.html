The Process

From idea to listing in four steps

01

Describe or Choose

Type a prompt or pick one of 12 StyleMood presets. The AI builds your first design instantly.

02

Customize

Swap elements, adjust colors, resize florals. The canvas responds like a professional design tool — but built for wreaths.

03

Check Your Numbers

The Cost Estimator tallies your material BOM in real time and suggests a retail price based on your margin target.

04

Export & List

Download your design files, product images, and listing copy — ready to publish on Etsy or Shopify in minutes.
