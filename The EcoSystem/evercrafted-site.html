Feelings become
design parameters.

The Memory-to-Masterpiece AI reads the emotional content of your client's words — grief, gratitude, nostalgia, celebration — and scores them by intensity, valence, and arousal. Those scores drive every downstream decision.

  • Emotion intensity scoring from 0–1 across 10+ emotional categories
  • Design glyphs extracted from the Fauxever Memories Lexicon
  • Poetic design story generated as a client-facing deliverable
  • Multiple AI provider support via the M2M provider registry

02 — Blueprint Engine

Every stem has
a reason.

The Blueprint Composition Engine uses FFLGA role classification, non-scoring floral selection with tier buckets, and golden-angle placement math to produce a design that's both structurally sound and emotionally intentional.

  • FFLGA: Focal / Filler / Line / Greenery / Accent role system
  • Non-scoring selection with diversity constraints and usage cooldown
  • Polar coordinate placements — every stem has (r, θ) coordinates
  • Optical balance scoring across all four canvas quadrants
03 — Genome System

Encode. Breed.
Evolve.

The Wreath Genome System encodes any design into a compact string — shareable, searchable, versioned. Mutate a color palette, breed two designs into a hybrid, or evolve a spring wreath into fall in one step.

  • WGS genome string: compact, human-readable design DNA
  • Encode / Decode / Mutate / Breed operations
  • Share a design as a single copiable string
  • Version your designs like code — fork, iterate, restore
