Motion types

Three modes of
physical expression

↔ Sway
01

Sway

The primary motion type. Simulates natural lateral movement with a subtle rotation component — the way a wreath responds to a gentle current of air. Feels weighted, physical, and organic.

Formula: x = sin(t) × amplitude
Rotation: sin(t) × rotation_factor
Best for: all seasonal designs, Etsy listings, social reels
↻ Rotation
02

Rotation

Simulated full 360° rotation with a depth-illusion shadow layer. Produces the most comprehensive view of the wreath design — every element visible from every angle.

Formula: angle = frame × (360 / total_frames)
Shadow: dynamic based on rotation phase
Best for: product showcases, bold editorial statement pieces
◎ Pulse
03

Pulse

A scale-based breathing effect — subtle expansion and contraction that creates a living, breathing presence. Best for romantic, soft, or meditative design palettes.

Formula: scale = 1 + sin(t) × intensity_factor
Opacity: coordinated with scale cycle
Best for: soft romantic designs, client portal previews
