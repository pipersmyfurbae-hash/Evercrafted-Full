The Process

From feeling to
finished blueprint

A four-stage pipeline that converts emotional intent into a production-ready wreath design — no guesswork, no wasted stems.

01

Capture the feeling

Your client doesn't describe flowers — they describe memories, moods, and occasions. The Client Design Intake Engine translates vague input into a structured brief: resolved color palette, stem counts, seasonal fit, and emotional intent.

60-30-10 Color Ratio Emotion Mapping Size Scaling
02

Generate a scored blueprint

The Blueprint Composition Engine places elements algorithmically using polar coordinates, cluster logic, and visual mass distribution rules. Every output is automatically scored and repaired before you see it.

Polar Coordinate Placement Balance Scoring Auto-Repair
03

Render the vision

The AI Prompt Compiler converts your blueprint into a Midjourney-ready render prompt — photoreal, styled, and locked to your WREATH_STYLE_DNA for brand-consistent outputs. No prompt engineering required.

Midjourney v7 Ready WREATH_STYLE_DNA Photoreal Output
04

Build with confidence

The Builder Instructions Generator converts your blueprint into step-by-step assembly notes — cluster order, stem counts, placement positions, and a cut list CSV. Hand it to any builder and the wreath comes out right.

Step-by-Step Assembly Cut List CSV Exact SKU References
