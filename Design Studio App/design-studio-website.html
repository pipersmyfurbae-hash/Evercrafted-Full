What it does

Design Studio — for wreaths that need to tell a story.

For simple wreaths, Layout Studio is enough. When a client asks for a wreath that transitions in mood or colour from one section to another — calm morning at the top, warm evening at the base — that's what Design Studio is built for.

Why this matters
Most design software doesn't understand that a wreath is a ring, not a canvas. Design Studio treats each arc of the ring as its own design zone — with its own emotional rules, density, and palette.

How it works

Simple to use from day one.

Three steps from blank canvas to client-ready blueprint.

01
Divide into zones
Split the ring into sections — romantic focal zone on the left, bold accent zone at the base, neutral fill zones between. Each zone has its own mood and rules.
02
Design zone by zone
Work through each section independently. Lock the zones you're happy with. Adjust the ones that aren't working. Apply a journey preset to instantly set the mood arc.
03
Export the full blueprint
When every zone is right, export the complete multi-section blueprint — zone boundaries, element positions, mood tags, and a clean client-ready PDF.
Features

Everything Design Studio can do.

Zone-by-zone control
Control each arc of the wreath independently — useful for complex custom designs where one section tells a completely different visual story to another.
Journey presets
Pre-built mood transitions — the design automatically shifts from one emotional tone to another across the wreath. Set it in one click.
Element locking
A client loves the big white peony at the bottom-left — lock it. Everything else adjusts around it. Locked elements survive preset changes and AI suggestions.
Client-ready export
Export a clean version with no technical overlays — a polished blueprint PDF your client can review and approve without needing to understand the system.
AI Blueprint Engine
Type a design intent. The AI engine outputs a complete multi-zone blueprint with element counts, emotion formula, palette direction, and quality scores.
Revision history
Every version of your design is saved automatically. Roll back to any point in the design process — or fork a previous version as a new design.
