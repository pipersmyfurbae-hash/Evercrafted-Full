How it works

From memory to
buildable blueprint

01
Tell it a memory
Describe a person, a place, a season, a feeling. Plain language is all it takes — no color pickers, no style questions.
02
Your words become a palette
Each feeling pulls toward different materials. Grief reaches for dark, still forms. Joy reaches outward toward light and warmth. The design starts to take shape before a single choice is made.
03
The design takes shape
Evercrafted builds the design in layers — the pieces that anchor it first, then the ones that soften and fill around them. A built-in intelligence ensures nothing repeats, so every design brings something genuinely new.
04
Render and build
You get the visual to show a client, the exact quantities to pull from your stock, and the steps to build it. Everything at once, ready to use.

A memory of Lisbon,
in early spring

Before a single floral is chosen, Evercrafted writes the scene — a piece that inhabits the memory rather than describing it. The kind of writing you could print on a card and send with the finished wreath. Every floral that follows traces back to what the poem carries.

Memory · Alfama, Lisbon · Spring · Morning

“The wreath hangs on the azulejo-tiled staircase wall as the first light spreads across the Tagus. Orange blossoms drift from unseen trees, mixing with the salt tang of the river. Even now, watching the light inch across the terracotta rooftops, there is the strange ache of missing this moment while still living inside it.”
