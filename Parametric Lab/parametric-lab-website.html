What it does

Parametric Lab turns mood into structure.

Instead of drawing your wreath shape by hand, Parametric Lab generates it from your choices. Pick a shape family — round, crescent, trailing, loose, or figure-eight — then dial in density, structure, scale, and asymmetry. The tool adjusts the proportions and layout to match how you want it to feel.

Why it matters
Unusual shapes are hard to plan by hand. Parametric Lab makes crescent, asymmetric, and trailing styles easy to explore without hours of trial and error — and exports directly to Layout Studio.
