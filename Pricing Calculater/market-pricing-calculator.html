2.5× is the minimum viable markup for a handmade product business to be sustainable. 3× is the professional standard for retail and wholesale viability. The calculator shows both so you can see the range and pick your positioning. A custom slider lets you explore any multiplier between them.
Overhead covers anything that costs money but doesn't show up as a direct material: packaging, shipping supplies, Etsy or platform fees (typically 6–12%), subscription costs, studio rent, and marketing. A starting overhead % of 10–15% is reasonable for most sellers; higher if you ship a lot or pay significant platform fees on every sale.
