Tagging quick reference
Role — what the item does in the wreath, not what it is
Movement — which direction does gravity pull it?
Finish — how does light interact with the surface?
Palette — color family, not specific color
Emotion — what does the combination of form + finish + palette feel like?
Intensity — 1 = gentle/muted, 2 = versatile, 3 = bold/architectural
Pass 1 items: structural, focal — inner wheel, heavy archetypes
Pass 2 items: secondary, texture, accent, greenery — outer ring, nuanced
