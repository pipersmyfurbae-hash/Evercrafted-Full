FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a Midjourney subscription?
EmotionLens generates the prompts — you paste them into Midjourney or any compatible AI image tool. A Midjourney subscription is separate and not included.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings with one click — no forms, no friction. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
What is a "brand preset"?
A saved combination of your signature emotions, palette, materials, and style settings. Load it instantly instead of reconfiguring each session.
