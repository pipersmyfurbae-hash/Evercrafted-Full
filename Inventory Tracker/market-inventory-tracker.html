How it fits your day

From Monday review to Thursday build

A simple weekly ritual that keeps your studio stocked and your projects on track.

1
Monday morning review

Open your tracker. Scan for amber and red. Know in 60 seconds what needs ordering before the week starts.

2
Order what's low

Filter to Low + Critical. Add to your wholesale cart. Check off items as you order. Stock levels update when they arrive.

3
Build your blueprint

When you generate a Blueprint, its materials list syncs to your tracker in one click. No double-entry, no guessing if you have enough.

4
Deduct on completion

At Studio tier, marking a blueprint complete automatically deducts its materials from your inventory. Your counts stay accurate without extra effort.
